Use this form to change your home address on your NHS record.

If your new address is outside of your current GP surgery's catchment area, your GP may contact you to confirm this. You might:

  • be able to stay with your current surgery
  • stay with your current surgery, but with some changes to the care you get
  • have to find a new GP surgery

If your current surgery doesn't accept patients outside of their catchment area, you will have 30 days to find a new GP to register with.

>
After you update your address

You may not be able to see your health information in the NHS App if you are not registered with a GP surgery after this. Once you have registered, you will just need to log out of the App and log back in to see this information again.

Before you start

Speak to your current GP if you:

  • are on a waiting list for elective surgery
  • have any GP appointments you are waiting for
  • take any prescription medications
