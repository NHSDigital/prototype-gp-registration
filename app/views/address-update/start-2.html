It's important the NHS has an up to date address so:

  • any NHS letters giving appointment dates or test results go to the right place
  • your GP surgery can provide home visits or other services

In most cases, when someone moves address they need to register with a new GP.

Registering with a GP surgery takes about 15 minutes and will update your NHS record with your new address.

Alternatively, choose 'Continue' to update your address here.

Information:

If you choose to continue it could result in you being removed from your current GP surgery.
