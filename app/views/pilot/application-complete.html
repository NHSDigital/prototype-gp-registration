What happens next

Your registration details will be sent to chosen GP so they can register you. You do not need to do anything else. Your health records will be transferred to your new GP surgery.

The time this takes varies between GP surgeries, usually no longer than 2 weeks.

If you need an appointment before then you can contact the practice to make one.

Blood and organ donation

Information:

On 20 May 2020, the law around organ donation in England was changed to allow more people to save more lives.

You still have a choice whether or not you want to become an organ donor, and can register or amend your decision at any time on Blood and transplant organ donation.
