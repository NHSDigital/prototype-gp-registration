Register with a GP surgery: {{ data.presets[0].gpsurgery or '{ GP surgery}' }}, Stratford-upon-Avon

Use this service to Use this service to register yourself or someone else with a GP surgery for the first time or to change a current GP surgery.

Do NOT use this service if you:

  • need medical help right now. Instead visit NHS 111 online (opens in a new tab).
  • need an urgent appointment. Instead you should contact your current GP using Find a GP (opens in a new tab).

If you are already registered with a GP surgery you can access some health and prescription services using NHS online services.

Before you start

If you have your NHS number it could make registration easier. Find your NHS number (opens in a new tab).

We may ask you for:

  • details of your previous GP surgery
  • basic health and medical information

The information you provide will be used to identify the correct medical records. It will also help the GP surgery to offer relevant and appropriate healthcare services.

Start now

How long it takes

It takes around 15 minutes to complete a registration. You need to complete the process in one session. If you are registering more than one person make sure to give yourself enough time.

Your chosen GP surgery should register you within 1 working day of receiving your details.

Other ways to register

  • Fill in the Register with a GP surgery paper form (PDF, 199 KB, 8 pages) and take it to the surgery you want to join.
  • Contact the surgery you want to join directly.

You can view guidance about how to register with a GP.
