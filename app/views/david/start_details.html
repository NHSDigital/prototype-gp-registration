Register with a GP surgery: {{ data.presets[0].gpsurgery or '{ GP surgery}' }}, Stratford-upon-Avon

Use this service to register yourself or someone else with a GP surgery for the first time or to transfer to a new GP surgery.

If you think you need medical help right now visit NHS 111 online.

If you are already registered with a GP surgery you can access some health and prescription services using NHS online services.

How long it takes

It takes around 15 minutes to complete a registration.

Your chosen GP surgery should register you within 5 days of receiving your details.

Before you start

If you have your NHS number, it could make registration easier.

Find your NHS number

Temporary registrations

You can temporarily register with a GP surgery for up to 3 months. This can be helpful if you're living away from home for a short period and do not want to change your existing GP surgery.

You should visit the GP surgery you want to register with and ask them for a temporary services form, also called a GMS3.

Find out about temporary registrations.

If you are registering more than one person

For multiple or family registrations you must complete and submit a registration for each person.

If you are registering children aged 15 years and under it is important that you register yourself first.

Start now

Other ways to register

  • Fill in the Register with a GP surgery paper form (PDF, 199 KB, 8 pages) and take it to the surgery you want to join.
  • Contact the surgery you want to join directly.

You can view guidance about how to register with a GP.
