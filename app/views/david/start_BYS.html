Register with a GP surgery: {{ data.presets[0].gpsurgery or '{ GP surgery}' }}, Stratford-upon-Avon

Use this service to register yourself or someone else with a GP surgery for the first time or to transfer to a new GP surgery.

Do not use this service if you:

  • need medical help right now. Instead visit NHS 111 online.
  • want to make an urgent appointment. Instead you should contact your current GP using Find a GP.
  • want a temporary registration of less than 3 months. Find out about temporary registrations.
  • are already registered with a GP surgery and want other NHS online health and prescription services.

Start now

Other ways to register

  • Fill in the Register with a GP surgery paper form (PDF, 199 KB, 8 pages) and take it to the surgery you want to join.
  • Contact the surgery you want to join directly.

You can view guidance about how to register with a GP.
