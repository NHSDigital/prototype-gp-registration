Further questions about your health

Thank you for confirming the details you have entered already.

To ensure you get the best possible care, we need to ask you some more questions about your health and lifestyle.

The things we'll ask about include:

  • your height and weight
  • any alcohol consumption
  • any tobacco usage
  • your medical conditions
  • your medications
  • your ethnicity

The process is confidential and any data entered is store securely following the UK government's data storage standards PDF, 2.25Mb

These questions are optional and do not have to be completed in order to register with a GP.
