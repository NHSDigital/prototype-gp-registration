Register with a GP

Use this service if you want to register with a GP. You can register for yourself or on behalf of a dependant in your care.

You do not have to have been previously registered with a GP to use this service.

Before you start

The registration process will take around 15 minutes, and we'll ask questions about the person being registered.

We need to ask these questions to help locate any previous medical records and ensure our that healthcare services are tailored to the needs of the person being registered.

The things we'll ask about include:

  • name and address
  • date and place of birth
  • NHS number, if you know it
  • previous GP surgeries, if there are any
  • health and medical information
  • contact details

The process is confidential and any data entered is store securely following the UK government's data storage standards PDF, 2.25Mb

You'll need to complete your application in one session, so please have these details to hand before you start.

Other ways to register

You can also register with a GP surgery by visiting them in person and completing a GMS1 registration form or other registration form. However, some surgeries may not be accepting visitors in person due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Call the surgery first for instructions.

You can view guidance about how to register with a GP or find a GP surgery using the Find a GP service
