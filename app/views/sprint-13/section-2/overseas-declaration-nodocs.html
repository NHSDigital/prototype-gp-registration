For people arriving from EU, EEA or Swtizerland without EHIC or S1 form.

By continuing you are agreeing to the following statements.

I understand that registration with  a GP surgery is free of charge and that I can receive free medical care from them.

I may need to pay for other NHS treatment, such as referrals to hospital, if:

  • I do not have a valid exemption such as an EHIC or an S1 form.

If you are from EU, EEA or Switzerland and you do not have an S1 form or an EHIC, you can find out how to get one and if you're eligible, from your national government websites. You can find out more information aboutUK healthcare for EU nationals on the Gov.uk website (Opens in new tab).
