How you choose to self-describe

You may choose to self-describe. Your GP surgery will use this information to help them improve the experience of everyone accessing primary care services.

For example, you may self-describe as:

  • Bisexual - someone who is attracted to men and women
  • Gay or lesbian - men who are attracted to men, and women who are attracted to women
  • Heterosexual (straight) - men who are attracted to women, and women who are attracted to men

If you prefer not to answer this question you can select 'Continue'.
