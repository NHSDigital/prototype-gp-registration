Blood transfusions

If you received a blood transfusion in the UK before 1996 there is a small chance you may have received infected blood.

Blood transfusions are routinely given to patients that:

  • have had an accident
  • have complications during childbirth
  • are receiving other medical treatment

Your GP surgery will use this information to manage your healthcare.
