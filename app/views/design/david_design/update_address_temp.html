Changing a temporary or correspondence address

Do not change your address using this service if it is a:

  • Temporary address - you'll live there for less than 3 months
  • Correspondence address - you only use the address for letters and post and do not normally live there

To change a temporary or correspondence address you should contact your nearest GP surgery.

Choose 'Continue' if you want to change your home address only.
