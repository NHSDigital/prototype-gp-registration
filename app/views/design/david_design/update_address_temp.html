Updating your home address

Your home address is where you normally live.

Do not update your address using this service if it is a:

  • Temporary address - you'll live there for less than 3 months
  • Correspondence address - you only use the address for letters and post and do not normally live there

To update a temporary or correspondence address you should contact your nearest GP surgery.
