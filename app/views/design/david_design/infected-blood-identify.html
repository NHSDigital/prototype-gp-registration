Infected Blood Inquiry

In response to the Infected Blood Inquiry, NHS England wants to identify anyone that may have received infected blood before the introduction of universal blood screening in 1992.

Information:

Evidence shows the likelihood of contracting a bloodborne illness through a blood transfusion after 1992 is extremely low following the introduction of universal blood screening to detect Hepatitis C infection in September 1991.

If you have any concerns you should contact your GP surgery directly.

The information you have provided will be shared with your GP surgery and they may offer you blood screening.
