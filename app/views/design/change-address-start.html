Changing your home address

Some GP surgeries will only accept patients that live within the area they cover. You can continue with this address change, but if you are outside the area covered by [[NAME OF SURGERY]] you will need to register with a new GP surgery.

If your new address is outside the area covered by your current surgery:

  • you may not be offered home visits
  • it could affect referrals for hospital tests and treatment, or community health services

You should speak to your GP surgery about your options.
