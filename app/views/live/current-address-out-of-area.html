Some GP surgeries will only accept patients that live within the area they cover. You can continue with this registration, but it may not be accepted.

Choosing a surgery outside your area means:

  • you may not be offered home visits
  • it could affect referrals for hospital tests and treatment, or community health services

You should speak to the GP surgery about your options, or find a surgery for this address using the Find a GP service.
