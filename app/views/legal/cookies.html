NHS Register with a GP Surgery cookies policy

NHS England ("we" or "us") uses cookies to deliver the “NHS Register with a GP Surgery” service

The information set out in this policy is provided in addition to the NHS Register with a GP Surgery privacy policy and should be read alongside it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files we put on your device (for example, your phone, tablet or laptop) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites and apps work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide services and functionalities for users.

How we use cookies

The “NHS Register with a GP Surgery” service uses two types of cookies:

  • Cookies we need to put on your device to make the service work and keep it secure. TheseWe are known as “strictly necessary cookies”.
  • Optional analytic cookies to collect information about how the service is used to help us improve it (sometimes referred to as "performance cookies" or “analytics cookies”.)
  • Analytics cookies help us to improve our service but we will only use them if you say it’s ok by selecting ‘Accept analytics cookies’ in the cookie pop-up when you visit the service.
    •  

Important:

If you're using a public computer, make sure you:

  • log out of the NHS App online
  • delete any health record documents you may have downloaded
  • delete any cookies from the web browser

Strictly necessary cookies we use

Name Purpose Expires
Name gp-reg-cookie-accept Purpose Persistent cookie. This cookie is set once a visitor has agreed to, or rejected cookies. It is used to save their decision on whether they have accepted or rejected cookies. Expires 365 days
Name aws-waf-token Purpose Amazon Web Services persistent cookie. This cookie contains encrypted information about the way you interact with our service which we use to establish that you are a legitimate visitor and protect our service from attacks using fake users. Expires 5 minutes

We also measure how many IP addresses connect to our web service with JavaScript disabled or use a browser which does not support JavaScript. We do this by using a technique called pixel tracking. We use this information to measure how accessible our service is and do not use it for any other purposes.

Your cookie choices

The majority of devices and browsers will allow you to the settings used for cookies and disable and enable them as you require.

You can select “reject analytics cookies” if you do not want these optional cookies placed on your device while you are using the service.

You can also delete the cookies already on your device, and you can set your browser or device to prevent them being placed.

Please be aware that if you set your preferences to not allow all cookies, it could limit the functionality of the “NHS Register with a GP Surgery” service as it needs the strictly necessary ones to work.

Changes to this cookie policy

Our terms of use, privacy policy and cookies policy may change. We'll inform you and request your continued agreement if we make any significant changes to our privacy policy, cookies policy or terms of use.
