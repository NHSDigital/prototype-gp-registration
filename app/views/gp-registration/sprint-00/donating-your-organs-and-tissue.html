Donating your organs and tissue

Organ donation is when you decide to give organs or tissue to someone else after death.

By registering your organ donation decision now, you consent to NHS England sharing your information with NHS Blood and Transplant to be recorded on the NHS Organ Donor Register. View their privacy statement (link opens in a new tab) to see how your data is used to support the NHS Organ Donor Register.

You can also register or update your decision at any time on the NHS Organ Donation website.
