Donating your organs and tissue

Organ donation is when you decide to give an organ or tissue to someone else. You can donate organs and tissue while you are alive or after you have died.

You do not have to make a decision right now. You can register your decision on the NHS organ donation website (link opens in a new tab) at any time.

The Register with a GP surgery service does not store any data related to your organ donation choices.
