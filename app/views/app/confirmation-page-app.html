Summary Care Record

When you register with a GP surgery a Summary Care Record (SCR) is created for you. It contains information about your medications, allergies and any adverse reactions.

You are free to change what's in your SCR at any time. To do this either:

  • complete a Summary Care Record Patient Consent Form (Word and Open Document format, opens in a new tab) and return it to your GP surgery, or
  • speak to your GP surgery about what's included in your SCR and what information you would want shared with authorised healthcare staff
