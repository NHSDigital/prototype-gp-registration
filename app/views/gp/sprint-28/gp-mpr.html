Manage patient registrations is a new feature that lets you:

  • view incoming patient registrations
  • review the information the patient gave in the online form
  • approve or reject patients
  • view details of patients you approved or rejected in the last 90 days

If you choose to turn on Manage patient registrations, you will also get access to auto registration.

Auto registration

Auto registration matches patients to their NHS record and creates a record for you in your clinical systems. It can help you save time and effort during the registration process. This is because you will not have to manually enter any information.

{% from "details/macro.njk" import details %} {% call details({ summaryText: "View patient criteria for auto registration" }) %}

These patients are:

  • aged 18 or older
  • in your practice catchment area
  • using NHS login to apply
  • transferring from another practice in England

They will not have a Special Allocation Scheme (SAS) marker, 1-day, or 8-day removal on their record.
