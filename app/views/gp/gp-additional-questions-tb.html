We will identify patients that:

  • are aged between 16-35
  • have recently moved to the UK, or
  • recently returned from overseas

They will be asked if have they spent more than 6 months in a country where there was an increased risk of catching TB.

Patients who answer yes to this question will be flagged to you as 'Likely to be eligible for tuberculosis screening'.
