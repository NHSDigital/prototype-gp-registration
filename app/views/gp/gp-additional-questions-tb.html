Do you want to ask patients if they are eligible for tuberculosis (TB) screening?

Patients who are aged between 16-35, have recently moved to the UK or recently returned from overseas, will be asked if they spent more than 6 months in a country where there was an increased risk of catching tuberculosis (TB)?

Patients who answer yes to this question will be flagged as 'likely to be eligible for tuberculosis screening' in their registration request email.
