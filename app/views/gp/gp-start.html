Sign up to Register with a GP surgery

The Register with a GP surgery service enables you to immediately start offering new patients the ability to register with your practice online through the new NHS service, 'Register with a GP Surgery'.

What happens next

  • select the surgery you want to sign up for the service
  • make sure you have access to a nhs.net shared mailbox, this is where patient registration details will be sent
  • select any additional services already provided by your surgery so that they can be offered to the patient during their registration process

Once you have signed up, you will receive a unique url web address to put on your surgery's website.

For more information about the service, visit the Resource hub (Opens in new tab).
