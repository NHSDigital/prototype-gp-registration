GP Registration Service

The GP Registration Service provides GP surgeries with the information they need to support them when registering new patients.

This information includes:

  • the details you need to register a patient
  • if the person registering has been matched to a record in Personal Demographics Service (PDS)
  • if they've used an NHS login when registering
  • important information about potentially vulnerable patients
