You need to complete this registration process if you want to offer the Register with a GP Surgery Online service to your patients.

What you need to do

  • answer all the questions in this registration process
  • register multiple practices individually

What we will do

Once you have completed this registration process we will:

  • create a unique registration url for your surgery webstie
  • update Find a gp (opens in new tab) with your unique registration link
  • apply your catchment area to the service
Information:

Registrations completed from 16 December 2022 will not be updated on Find a GP until after 3 January 2023 because of the holiday break.

More information about using the service

A resource hub (opens in new tab) has been made available which explains how you can:

  • promote the service in your surgery
  • get support while using the service
