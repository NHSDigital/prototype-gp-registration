Send registration success messages

Messages are automatically sent to patients telling them their registration with your surgery has been successful. The messages are sent by email, text message, post or in the NHS App.

These messages are sent as part of the Register with a GP surgery service, unless you choose to turn them off. You can turn them on again at any time through NHS Profile Manager.
